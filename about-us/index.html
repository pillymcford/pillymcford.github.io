About Us

We are a locally owned and operated construction company servicing Bozeman, MT and surrounding areas. We offer a wide verity of services from general contracting new construction and remodels, to fully in house jobs including roofing, siding, and concrete work. McLane Construction is a licensed and insured construction company and has a current business license to work in Bozeman, Belgrade, Livingstone and West Yellowstone.
