About Us

We are a locally owned and operated construction company servicing Bozeman, MT and surrounding areas. We offer a wide verity of services from general contracting new construction and remodels, to fully in house jobs including roofing, siding, and concrete work. McLane Construction is a licensed and insured construction company and has a current business license to work in Bozeman, Belgrade, Livingstone and West Yellowstone. All of our employees are insured as well as our subcontractors. This protects our business as well as our clients from ending up with damaging claims. We take compliance with regulations, business transparency, and attention to safety very seriously because it is crucial to our sustainability as a company. We take pride in our work because we are members of this community; our reputation depends on our relationship with our clients here in Montana. Our business also depends on delivering a quality product, one that will last and do the job right. We stand behind our work because it is our responsibility to our clients and we are confident in our attention to detail and our craftsmanship.
